Impact of coronavirus on Indian Economy

India’s economic growth could take a hit of up to half a percentage point in FY21 because of the disruptions caused by the Covid-19 outbreak, early estimates by the government suggest. But independent economists see a deeper cut of up to one percentage point. “There will be a hit of 0.3-0.5% on the GDP in the next fiscal year,” said one of the officials aware of the estimate. “Growth in the first two quarters of the next fiscal could be as low as 4-4.5%,” another official added....

Sensex soars over 600 points

Sensex jumped over 600 points in early trade on Tuesday while the broader Nifty regained 9,300-mark even as metal, pharma and FMCG sectors witnessing buying interest, while selling was seen in the bank, infra and IT stocks.
